Diabetic Alert Service Dog Training

The training program for Diabetic Alert Dogs includes all of the Basic Obedience Skills, Canine Good Citizenship, basic service dog skills, as well as alert skills and series skills to bring supplies to the handler. Training alerts will also require collection and mailing of saliva samples of both low and high blood sugar levels several times through the training process.

  • Diabetic Alert Skills
  • Scratching at side of leg while standing
  • Front paws on lap or legs while seated
  • Bark when no response to earlier alerts
  • Finding someone to assist if unresponsive
  • Skills to perform in series
  • Open door
  • Pick it up
  • Close Door
  • Bring it
  • Drop it
  • Scratch at leg
  • Paws on leg
  • Bark
  • Find someone
