Service Dog Training

All of the Service Dog Training programs include the following skills. These skills are in taught in addition to the Basic Obedience Skills and the Good Citizenship training and test.

  • Basic Service Dog Training
  • Touching a designated spot with either paw or nose
  • Picking up a designated item
  • Bring a designated item to you
  • Pushing a button or light switch
  • Putting front paws on your lap or legs
  • Barking on cue
  • Sitting between legs when not moving
  • Pushing a doorbell next to door to go outside
  • Open a light door using a pull
  • Pushing a door closed
