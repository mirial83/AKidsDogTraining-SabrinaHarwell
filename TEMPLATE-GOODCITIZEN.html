Canine Good Citizenship Training and Test

The Canine Good Citizenship program includes all of the skills in the Basic Obedience program plus the following skills. This program also includes taking the Canine Good Citizenship test, which is charged in addition to the basic training fees.

  • Canine Good Citzenship Skills
  • Accepting a friendly stranger
  • Sitting politely for petting
  • Walking through a crowd
  • Sit, Down, and Stay
  • Polite reaction to another dog
  • No reaction to distractions such as children playing, walkers, canes, crutches, etc
  • Remaining calm while supervised by a stranger
